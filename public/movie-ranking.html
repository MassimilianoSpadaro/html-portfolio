The Best Movies According to Massimiliano

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Potter

This is my favourite movie saga. Harry Potter is the story of a teenage wizard who's been raised by his non-magical aunt and uncle.

Big Fish

American fantasy drama film directed by Tim Burton, and based on the 1998 novel Big Fish.

Lion

It tells the true story of how Brierley, 25 years after being separated from his family in India, sets out to find them.
